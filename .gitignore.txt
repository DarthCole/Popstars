Dream: Popstars

Synopsis: Popstar Hub is a stylized, pop culture inspired multiplayer game that combines a collection of mini-games into a single interactive experience centered around music, performance, and competition. Players enter a vibrant hub world where they can choose from a variety of game modes, earn in-game currency (StarCoins), and customize their popstar identity.

Overall, Popstar Hub aims to deliver a fun, expressive, and competitive experience that blends casual gameplay with the excitement of popstar culture, allowing players to perform, customize, and battle their way to stardom.
